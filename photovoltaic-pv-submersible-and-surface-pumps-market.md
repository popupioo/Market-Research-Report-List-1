<p><p>The photovoltaic (PV) pumps market is divided into two primary types: submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to be submerged in water and are typically used for deep well applications, efficiently lifting water to the surface powered by solar energy. Surface pumps, on the other hand, operate above ground, drawing water from a source like a river or tank. Both types optimize energy consumption, reduce operational costs, and promote sustainability in water management.</p></p>
