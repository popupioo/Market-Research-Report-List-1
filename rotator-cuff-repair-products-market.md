<p><p>The rotator cuff repair products market features various types of suture anchors designed for orthopedic surgeries. PLA resorbable suture anchors provide temporary fixation that gradually dissolves, promoting healing without leaving permanent material. Biocomposite suture anchors combine biocompatibility with durability. PEEK suture anchors are strong and radiolucent, minimizing interference in imaging. Metal suture anchors offer robustness but may pose visibility issues. All-suture anchors are lightweight, using only sutures for fixation. PEEK carbon fiber suture anchors blend strength with reduced visibility, enhancing tissue integration.</p></p>
