<p><p>The stationary barcode scanner market includes three main types: laser scanners, linear imagers, and 2D imager scanners. Laser scanners use laser beams to read barcode patterns, providing fast and accurate scanning for traditional 1D barcodes. Linear imagers capture images of barcodes and decode them, ideal for consistent scanning in retail environments. 2D imager scanners offer advanced functionality by reading both 1D and 2D barcodes, including QR codes, making them versatile for various applications, from logistics to retail.</p></p>
