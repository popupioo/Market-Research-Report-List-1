<p><p>The modified wood decking market primarily comprises two types: thermally modified wood and chemically modified wood. Thermally modified wood undergoes a heat treatment process that enhances durability, resistance to moisture, and dimensional stability, making it suitable for outdoor use. In contrast, chemically modified wood is treated with preservatives or additives that improve resistance to pests and decay while maintaining its natural appearance. Both types cater to environmentally conscious consumers seeking sustainable, long-lasting decking solutions.</p></p>
