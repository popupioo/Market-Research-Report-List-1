<p><p>The microkeratome market comprises two main types: reusable and disposable. Reusable microkeratomes are designed for multiple uses, offering cost-effectiveness over time while typically requiring sterilization after each use. On the other hand, disposable microkeratomes are single-use tools that eliminate the need for sterilization and reduce the risk of cross-contamination, making them convenient and safe. Both types cater to the needs of eye surgeons and clinics, depending on their operational preferences and patient safety protocols.</p></p>
