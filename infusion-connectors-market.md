<p><p>The infusion connectors market comprises various types, including Y infusion connectors, T infusion connectors, and straight infusion connectors. Y infusion connectors enable simultaneous medication administration, enhancing efficiency in IV therapy. T infusion connectors allow the branching of multiple lines into a single connection, facilitating multiple therapies. Straight infusion connectors provide a direct connection between IV lines and devices, ensuring a streamlined flow of fluids. Collectively, these connectors enhance patient safety and treatment effectiveness in medical settings.</p></p>
