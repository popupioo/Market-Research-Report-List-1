<p><p>The De-aromatic Solvent Oil market is categorized by flash points, which determine the safety and suitability of solvents for various applications. Low flash point oils, typically below 38°C, are used in processes requiring rapid evaporation but pose higher flammability risks. Medium flash point oils, ranging from 38°C to 60°C, strike a balance between safety and effectiveness, making them popular in industrial applications. High flash point oils, above 60°C, offer increased safety and stability, ideal for high-temperature processes or environments with stringent safety regulations.</p></p>
