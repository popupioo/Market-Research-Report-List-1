<p><p>Fluid rotary unions are crucial components in applications requiring rotational movement while transmitting fluids. The market features three primary types: single channel, double channel, and multi-channel. Single channel unions facilitate a single fluid flow path, ideal for simpler systems. Double channel unions allow for two fluid flows concurrently, enhancing efficiency in more complex machinery. Multi-channel unions support multiple fluid streams, catering to advanced applications in industries like aerospace and robotics, where simultaneous fluid transfer is essential for operational performance.</p></p>
