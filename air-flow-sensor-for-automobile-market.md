<p><ul><li>Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor</li><li>Volumetric Air Flow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile air flow sensor market comprises primarily two types: the Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensor and the Volumetric Air Flow sensor. The MAF sensor measures the amount of air entering the engine, providing critical data to the engine control unit for optimal fuel injection and combustion efficiency. In contrast, the Volumetric Air Flow sensor focuses on the air volume rather than mass, offering insights into air intake dynamics. Both sensors are vital for enhancing vehicle performance and reducing emissions.</p></p>
