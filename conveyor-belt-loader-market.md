<p><strong>The Conveyor Belt Loader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled</li><li>Towable Models</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conveyor Belt Loader market is categorized into two main types: self-propelled models and towable models. Self-propelled conveyor belt loaders are equipped with their own propulsion system, allowing them to move independently, which enhances operational efficiency. Towable models, on the other hand, are designed to be moved by another vehicle, making them suitable for applications where mobility is needed but the load capacity is lower. Both types are essential for efficient cargo handling in airports and freight operations.</p></p>
