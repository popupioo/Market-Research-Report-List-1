<p><p>The textile colorant market comprises various types, primarily including acid colorants, alkaline colorants, and other specialty colorants. Acid colorants are typically used for protein fibers like wool and silk, offering bright hues and good wash fastness. Alkaline colorants, on the other hand, are suitable for cellulosic fibers such as cotton and are effective in alkaline dyeing processes. Other market segments include disperse colorants for synthetic fibers, reactive colorants for cotton, and other innovative dye technologies catering to diverse textile applications.</p></p>
