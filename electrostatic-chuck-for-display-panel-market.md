<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are essential in the display panel market for precise wafer handling. Coulomb-type ESCs utilize electrical forces to hold substrates, offering simplicity and reliability, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type ESC employs a combination of electrostatic and magnetic forces, providing enhanced grip and stability, especially for larger or heavier panels. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient production and improved quality in display technologies.</p></p>
