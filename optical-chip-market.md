<p><ul><li>Optical Active Chip</li><li>Optical Passive Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical chip market is primarily divided into two types: optical active chips and optical passive chips. Optical active chips are devices that amplify or regenerate optical signals, playing a crucial role in signal processing for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, optical passive chips do not require external power to operate; they rely on passive components like waveguides and filters to manage and route light signals. Both types are essential for advancing optical communication technologies and enhancing data transmission efficiency.</p></p>
