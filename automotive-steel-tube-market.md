<p><strong>The Automotive Steel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Welded Steel Tube</li><li>Seamless Steel Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steel tube market comprises two primary types: welded steel tubes and seamless steel tubes. Welded steel tubes are produced by bending and welding flat steel strips, making them cost-effective and suitable for various applications, such as chassis and exhaust systems. In contrast, seamless steel tubes are formed without seams, offering higher strength and durability, making them ideal for critical components under high pressure, like fuel lines and hydraulic systems. Both types serve essential roles in automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
