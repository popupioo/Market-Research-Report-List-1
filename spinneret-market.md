<p><strong>The Spinneret Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Melt Spinning Spinneret</li><li>Wet Spinneret</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spinneret markets are categorized primarily into melt spinning and wet spinning types, each corresponding to distinct fiber production processes. Melt spinning utilizes a spinneret to extrude molten polymer, cooling it to form fibers, commonly used in thermoplastic materials. In contrast, wet spinning involves dissolving polymers in a solvent, then extruding the solution through a spinneret into a coagulating bath, solidifying the fibers. Both types cater to various textile applications, influencing the properties and performance of the resulting fibers.</p></p>
