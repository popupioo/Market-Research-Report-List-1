<p><ul><li>Panel Mount</li><li>PCB Mount</li><li>DIN Rail Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid relay market is categorized into three main types: panel mount, PCB mount, and DIN rail mount. Panel mount relays are designed for installation on control panels, providing easy access and maintenance. PCB mount relays are compact and suitable for direct integration onto printed circuit boards, ideal for space-constrained applications. DIN rail mount relays are designed for quick installation on standard DIN rails, commonly used in industrial settings for their modularity and ease of wiring. Each type serves specific installation needs in diverse applications.</p></p>
