<p><p>The Diesel Pour Point Depressant (PPD) market features two main types: Alkylaromatics and Aliphatic Polymers. Alkylaromatics are chemical additives that enhance the fluidity of diesel at low temperatures, effectively lowering the pour point by disrupting wax formation. Aliphatic Polymers, on the other hand, are long-chain compounds that improve flow characteristics in cold conditions by modifying the wax crystal structure. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the efficient performance of diesel fuels in colder climates.</p></p>
