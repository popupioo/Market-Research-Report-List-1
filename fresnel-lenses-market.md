<p><p>The Fresnel lenses market can be categorized into two main types: imaging and non-imaging. Imaging Fresnel lenses are designed for precise focusing and are commonly used in applications like photography, projectors, and optical instruments, where clarity and detail are paramount. In contrast, non-imaging Fresnel lenses are utilized for light collection and redistribution, often found in solar concentrators and lighting systems, optimizing light without the need for a focused image. Both types are essential in various industries, including optics and renewable energy.</p></p>
