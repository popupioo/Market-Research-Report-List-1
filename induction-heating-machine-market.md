<p><ul><li>High-Frequency Induction Heating Machine</li><li>Medium-Frequency Induction Heating Machine</li><li>Ultra-High-Frequency Induction Heating Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The induction heating machine market is categorized into three main types based on frequency. High-frequency induction heating machines, operating around 100 kHz to several MHz, are typically used for surface hardening and brazing. Medium-frequency machines, ranging from 1 kHz to 100 kHz, are suitable for applications like forging and melting of metals. Ultra-high-frequency induction heating machines, exceeding 1 MHz, are utilized in specialized processes with precise heating requirements, such as in microelectronics and advanced materials processing.</p></p>
