<p><p>Operating Room Laminar Flow Modules are designed to maintain aseptic conditions during surgical procedures by providing a continuous flow of filtered air. The market can be categorized into horizontal and vertical flow types. Horizontal laminar flow modules direct airflow laterally across the operating area, ensuring a clean environment by sweeping away contaminants. Conversely, vertical laminar flow modules deliver air from above, creating a downward airflow that also minimizes particles. Both types are crucial in enhancing surgical outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
