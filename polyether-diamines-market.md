<p><p>The Polyether Diamines market encompasses two primary types based on their chemical backbone: PPG (propylene glycol) and PEG (polyethylene glycol). PPG backbone diamines are known for their versatility and are often used in applications like coatings, adhesives, and sealants due to their enhanced moisture resistance and chemical stability. Conversely, PEG backbone diamines exhibit lower toxicity and greater biocompatibility, making them suitable for applications in pharmaceuticals and personal care products. Both types are crucial for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
