<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasonic Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental ultrasonic scaler market comprises two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric ultrasonic scalers. Magnetostrictive scalers utilize a stack of metal strips to produce vibrations, effectively removing calculus and plaque through water spray. In contrast, piezoelectric scalers rely on ceramic crystals that expand and contract to generate ultrasonic waves, offering precise control and less heat production. Both scalers enhance dental hygiene practices, improving patient comfort and treatment outcomes through efficient scaling techniques.</p></p>
