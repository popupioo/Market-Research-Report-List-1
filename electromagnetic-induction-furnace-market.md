<p><ul><li>Coreless Electromagnetic Induction Furnace</li><li>Channel Electromagnetic Induction Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic induction furnace market is primarily divided into two types: coreless electromagnetic induction furnaces and channel electromagnetic induction furnaces. Coreless furnaces utilize electromagnetic fields to heat and melt the metal directly within a crucible, allowing for efficient energy use and quick temperature control. In contrast, channel furnaces allow molten metal to flow through a channel, facilitating continuous processing. Both types cater to various industrial applications, including foundries and steel production, emphasizing efficiency, scalability, and precise metalworking capabilities.</p></p>
