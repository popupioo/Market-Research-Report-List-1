<p><p>The de-icing products market encompasses three main types: fluid, solid, and granular de-icing products. Fluid de-icing products are typically liquid applications designed to quickly lower freezing points and melt ice on surfaces. Solid de-icing products involve compounds like salt that can be spread directly onto icy areas. Granular de-icing products consist of larger particles, offering slower but longer-lasting melting effects. Each type serves specific needs in environments affected by ice and snow, enhancing safety and accessibility.</p></p>
