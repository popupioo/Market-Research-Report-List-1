<p><p>The single drum vibratory roller market comprises two main types: walking type and driving type. The walking type features a static operator who guides the machine, offering better control in confined spaces and uneven terrains. In contrast, the driving type allows the operator to remain seated and operate the roller, enhancing efficiency and speed over larger, open areas. Both types are essential for compaction tasks in construction, road maintenance, and landscaping, catering to different operational needs and site conditions.</p></p>
