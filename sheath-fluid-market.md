<p><strong>The Sheath Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single sheath flow</li><li>Multi-sheath flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sheath fluid market is divided into two main types: single sheath flow and multi-sheath flow. Single sheath flow utilizes one fluid layer to focus and direct particles in a stream, enhancing precision in applications like flow cytometry. In contrast, multi-sheath flow involves multiple layers of sheath fluids, allowing for improved separation and manipulation of particles, which enhances resolution and sensitivity. Both types are used across various industries, including biotechnology and pharmaceuticals, to optimize fluid dynamics and analysis.</p></p>
