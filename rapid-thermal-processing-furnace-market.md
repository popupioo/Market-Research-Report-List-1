<p><strong>The Rapid Thermal Processing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) furnace market consists of two primary types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTP furnaces utilize high-intensity lamps to provide rapid heating, offering uniform temperature control and quick thermal cycles, which are ideal for semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, laser-based RTP systems employ focused laser beams for localized heating, enabling precise thermal treatment of specific areas without significantly affecting surrounding regions. Both technologies enhance process efficiency and improve material properties in advanced manufacturing applications.</p></p>
