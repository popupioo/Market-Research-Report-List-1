<p><p>Candy packaging machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and functionality. Vertical packaging machines operate by dispensing packaging materials from the top down, offering efficient space utilization and suitability for lightweight products. In contrast, horizontal packaging machines work by moving products along a horizontal plane, providing better handling for larger or more complex candies. Both types serve unique needs in the candy industry, ensuring efficient packaging while enhancing product appeal and shelf life.</p></p>
