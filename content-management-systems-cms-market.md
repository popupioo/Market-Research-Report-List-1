<p><p>Content Management Systems (CMS) are software applications that enable users to create, manage, and edit digital content without requiring technical expertise. The market for CMS is divided into two primary types: web-based and on-premises. Web-based CMS are hosted in the cloud, offering flexibility, ease of access, and reduced maintenance needs, making them ideal for businesses seeking scalability. On-premises CMS, however, are installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing greater control over security, customization, and data management, suitable for organizations with specific compliance needs.</p></p>
