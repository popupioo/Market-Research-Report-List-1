<p><p>Wireless Power Controller ICs are essential components in the wireless charging market, comprising two main types: transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs regulate power transmission from a charging station to devices, optimizing efficiency and ensuring safety. In contrast, receiver ICs convert the transmitted power into usable energy for the device, managing the charging process and protecting against overcharging. Together, these ICs facilitate seamless wireless charging experiences across various consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
