<p><p>The Medical Airflow Sensor market consists of several types, each designed for specific applications. Vane airflow sensors utilize mechanical components to measure airflow, ideal for higher flow rates. Karman vortex sensors detect airflow by analyzing vortex patterns created by an obstacle, suitable for varied environments. Hot wire sensors use a heated wire to determine airflow based on cooling effects, preferred for precision. Hot film sensors operate similarly but employ a film element, offering fast response times and excellent sensitivity in medical settings.</p></p>
