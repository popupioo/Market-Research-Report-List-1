<p><p>The zinc-ion battery market encompasses several types, each utilizing zinc as a key component. Nickel-zinc batteries offer high energy density and are rechargeable. Zinc-chloride batteries provide improved energy capacity over traditional alkaline versions. Collectively, these technologies highlight the versatility and potential of zinc-ion batteries.</p></p>
