<p><p>The eyelash enhancing serum market is primarily categorized into two types: formulas containing prostaglandins and those with peptides. Prostaglandin-based serums are designed to promote lash growth by mimicking natural growth factors, resulting in longer, thicker lashes. On the other hand, peptide-infused serums utilize amino acids to stimulate hair follicle activity and strengthen existing lashes, promoting healthier growth. Both types cater to consumers seeking effective solutions for enhancing lash appearance, but they differ in their mechanisms and potential side effects.</p></p>
