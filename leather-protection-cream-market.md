<p><strong>The Leather Protection Cream Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leather protection cream market can be categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based creams are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and easy to clean, making them ideal for casual use and regular maintenance. They penetrate the leather while leaving minimal residue. In contrast, solvent-based creams provide a more durable protective layer, enhancing water resistance and longevity but may contain harsher chemicals. These are often preferred for high-wear applications or outdoor leather products due to their robust performance under challenging conditions.</p></p>
