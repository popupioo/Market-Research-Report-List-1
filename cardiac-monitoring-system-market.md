<p><p>The cardiac monitoring system market comprises several key types, including Holter monitors, which provide continuous ECG recording for up to 48 hours; implantable loop recorders that monitor heart rhythms long-term; cardiac output monitoring devices that assess heart function and blood flow; and cardiac rhythm management devices (CRM) which include pacemakers and defibrillators, used for treating arrhythmias. These devices collectively enhance patient care by enabling early detection, diagnosis, and management of various cardiac conditions.</p></p>
