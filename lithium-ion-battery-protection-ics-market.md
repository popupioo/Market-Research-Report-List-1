<p><p>The Lithium-Ion Battery Protection ICs market is categorized primarily into two types: single-cell protection ICs and multi-cell protection ICs. Single-cell protection ICs are designed for individual lithium-ion cells, ensuring safety features such as overvoltage, undervoltage, and short-circuit protection. In contrast, multi-cell protection ICs manage multiple cells in series, providing comprehensive monitoring and protection across entire battery packs. Both types are essential for enhancing battery safety, extending life cycles, and ensuring reliable performance in various applications.</p></p>
