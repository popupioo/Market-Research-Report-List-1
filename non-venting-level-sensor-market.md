<p><strong>The Non-Venting Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Type Liquid Level Sensor</li><li>Hydrostatic Liquid Level Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Venting Level Sensor market includes Float Type Liquid Level Sensors and Hydrostatic Liquid Level Sensors. Float Type Sensors use a buoyant float attached to a rod or cable to detect liquid levels by moving with the liquid's surface. Hydrostatic Liquid Level Sensors, on the other hand, measure pressure at the bottom of a tank, determining fluid levels based on the weight of the liquid column above. Both types offer reliable, maintenance-free solutions for various liquid level sensing applications.</p></p>
