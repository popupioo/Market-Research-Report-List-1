<p><p>The active hearing protection market includes two main types: earplugs and earmuffs. Earplugs are compact, portable devices that fit snugly in the ear canal, providing effective noise reduction while allowing for communication and situational awareness. Earmuffs are larger, covering the entire ear and often incorporating electronic components to selectively block harmful noise while amplifying softer sounds. Both types are essential for individuals in noisy environments, enhancing safety and comfort without sacrificing communication capabilities.</p></p>
