<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) market is divided into two primary types: Area Scan and Line Scan. Area Scan SWIR cameras capture entire scenes in a single image, making them suitable for applications like industrial inspection and surveillance. In contrast, Line Scan SWIR cameras capture images one line at a time, providing high resolution over elongated areas, ideal for continuous processes like web inspection in manufacturing. Both types leverage SWIR technology to enhance imaging in challenging conditions, such as low light or obscured environments.</p></p>
