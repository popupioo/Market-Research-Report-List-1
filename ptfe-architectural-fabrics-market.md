<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PTFE Architectural Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE Fabric</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE architectural fabrics market comprises two primary types: porous and non-porous PTFE fabrics. Porous PTFE fabrics allow air and moisture to pass through, making them suitable for applications requiring ventilation, such as tensile structures and shading solutions. In contrast, non-porous PTFE fabrics are impermeable, providing excellent weather resistance and durability, ideal for cladding and roofing applications. Both types offer exceptional UV resistance, easy maintenance, and longevity, making them popular choices in modern architectural designs.</p></p>
