<p><strong>The Refractory Clay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Clay</li><li>Soft Clay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory clay market is categorized into two primary types: hard clay and soft clay. Hard clay, characterized by its high-density and strength, is primarily used in applications requiring durability, such as kiln linings and casting. In contrast, soft clay, which is more pliable and easier to mold, is utilized in products like bricks and ceramics. Both types play critical roles in various industries, including metallurgy, ceramics, and construction, offering specific properties tailored to meet diverse processing needs.</p></p>
