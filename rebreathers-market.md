<p><p>The rebreathers market primarily consists of two main types: closed circuit rebreathers and semi-closed rebreathers. Closed circuit rebreathers recycle exhaled gas, removing carbon dioxide and adding fresh oxygen, enabling longer dives with reduced gas consumption. Due to their efficiency, they're popular among technical divers. In contrast, semi-closed rebreathers allow a portion of exhaled gas to escape, providing a simpler design, often used for recreational diving. Each type caters to different user needs, from technical exploration to leisure activities.</p></p>
