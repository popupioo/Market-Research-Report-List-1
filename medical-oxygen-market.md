<p><p>The Medical Oxygen Market comprises two primary types: Gaseous State and Liquid State. Gaseous oxygen is stored in high-pressure cylinders and is commonly used in hospitals, clinics, and home care for respiratory therapies and emergencies. Liquid oxygen, stored in cryogenic tanks, is more efficient for large-scale storage and transportation, often used in hospitals with high oxygen demand. Both forms are critical for treating respiratory conditions, surgeries, and critical care, with liquid oxygen offering higher purity and storage capacity.</p></p>
