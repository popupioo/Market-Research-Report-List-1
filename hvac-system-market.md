<p><p>The HVAC system market encompasses various heating solutions, including heat pumps, furnaces, boilers, and unitary heaters. Heat pumps transfer heat for both heating and cooling, offering energy efficiency. Furnaces generate warmth through combustion, typically using natural gas or electricity. Boilers produce hot water or steam for heating applications, ideal for larger spaces. Unitary heaters are self-contained units providing localized heating. Each type serves distinct needs, contributing to diverse applications across residential and commercial sectors, driving market growth.</p></p>
