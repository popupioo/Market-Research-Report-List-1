<p><p>The UHF Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) inlay market is primarily categorized into two types: UHF dry inlays and UHF wet inlays. UHF dry inlays consist of a chip and antenna embedded in a paper or film substrate, suitable for applications where flexibility and thinness are essential, such as in labels. In contrast, UHF wet inlays are encapsulated in a more robust material, making them ideal for challenging environments and reusable applications, often used in asset tracking and supply chain management.</p></p>
