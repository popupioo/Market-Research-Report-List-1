<p><p>The RF & Microwave Ceramic Capacitors market is primarily divided into two types: Class 1 and Class 2 ceramic capacitors. Class 1 capacitors offer stable performance with low dielectric loss, making them ideal for precision applications where temperature stability is crucial. In contrast, Class 2 capacitors provide higher capacitance values and are generally more compact, but they exhibit greater variations in capacitance with temperature and voltage. Both types serve critical roles in high-frequency circuits, each catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
