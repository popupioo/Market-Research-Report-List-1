<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED solar street lights market can be categorized into two main types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar street lights operate independently, utilizing solar panels and batteries to function without connection to the electrical grid. They are ideal for remote areas, providing flexibility and ease of installation. In contrast, grid-connected solar street lights are linked to the electrical grid, allowing them to draw energy when solar power is insufficient. These systems are suitable for urban environments, enhancing energy efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
