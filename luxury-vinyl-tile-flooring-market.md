<p><p>The luxury vinyl tile flooring market comprises three primary types: SPC (Stone Plastic Composite), WPC (Wood Plastic Composite), and others. SPC features a rigid core made from a blend of limestone and PVC, offering durability and moisture resistance. WPC combines wood fibers and PVC, providing a softer underfoot feel and enhanced insulation. Other types may include traditional vinyl tiles and specialty variations. These options cater to diverse aesthetic and functional preferences, appealing to various consumer needs in both residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
