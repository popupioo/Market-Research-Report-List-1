<p><p>The Hepatitis B Infectious Disease Tests market comprises three primary testing types: Antibody tests, Antigen tests, and Nucleic Acid Testing (NAT). Antibody tests detect the immune response to the virus, indicating past or current infection. Antigen tests identify the presence of the virus itself, revealing active infection. Nucleic Acid Testing directly detects viral genetic material, providing the most sensitive means for diagnosing Hepatitis B and monitoring treatment. Together, these tests enhance the understanding and management of the disease.</p></p>
