<p><p>Electronic Data Capture (EDC) systems encompass three primary market types: web-hosted, licensed enterprise, and cloud-based solutions. Web-hosted EDC systems are typically accessible via the internet, requiring minimal local hardware. Licensed enterprise systems involve purchasing software licenses, often installed on-premises, offering greater control but higher initial costs. Cloud-based EDC systems operate on a subscription model, enabling scalable and flexible access to data from anywhere while reducing IT maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and resource availability.</p></p>
