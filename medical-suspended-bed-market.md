<p><p>The Medical Suspended Bed market features two primary types: Sand Suspended Beds and Air Pulsating Suspension Beds. Sand Suspended Beds utilize a layer of fine sand to evenly distribute a patient's weight, providing enhanced comfort and reducing pressure sores. In contrast, Air Pulsating Suspension Beds use air-filled chambers that alternately inflate and deflate to create a dynamic support surface, promoting blood circulation and preventing skin breakdown. Both types aim to improve patient care and comfort in medical settings.</p></p>
