<p><p>The indoor treadmill market is categorized into motorised and non-motorised treadmills. Motorised treadmills are equipped with electric motors that provide adjustable speed and incline settings, making them ideal for various workout intensities. They often come with advanced features like heart rate monitors and built-in workout programs. In contrast, non-motorised treadmills rely on the user’s effort to power the belt, promoting a more natural running experience and often being lightweight and easier to store. Both types offer unique benefits to fitness enthusiasts.</p></p>
