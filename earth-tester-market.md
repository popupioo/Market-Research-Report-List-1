<p><ul><li>Bench Top Earth Tester</li><li>Clamp on Earth Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Earth Tester market is categorized into two main types: Bench Top Earth Testers and Clamp-on Earth Testers. Bench Top Earth Testers are stationary devices designed for laboratory or workshop use, providing precise resistance measurements of grounding systems. They typically offer advanced features and higher accuracy. In contrast, Clamp-on Earth Testers are portable devices that allow technicians to measure earth resistance without disconnecting the grounding system, making them ideal for on-site inspections and quick assessments in various applications.</p></p>
