<p><p>The microwave power meters market comprises three main types: thermal power meters, diode power meters, and others. Thermal power meters function by converting microwave energy into heat, accurately measuring power across a wide range. Diode power meters utilize semiconductor diodes that respond to microwaves, offering fast response times and compact designs, ideal for portable applications. Other types include those employing advanced technologies like calorimetric or RF sensors, catering to specific customer requirements and varied measurement scenarios in the microwave spectrum.</p></p>
